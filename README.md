## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to know how to code?
**No!** This guide is specifically designed for non-coders. AI agents will generate the code based on your natural language descriptions.

### Is there a fee to participate?
**No!** The hackathon is completely free. Hedera testnet accounts and HBAR are free. All recommended tools have free tiers.

### Can I work alone or do I need a team?
**Both!** You can participate as an individual or form teams of up to 7 members.

### How do I register for the hackathon?
**Each team member must register individually** on the [DoraHacks platform](https://dorahacks.io/hackathon/hederahackafrica?utm_source=other&utm_medium=partnership&utm_campaign=default&utm_code=68f7c4cc72be979bf88a407455d88478). However, only the team lead submits the final project on behalf of the team.

### What if I'm not from Africa?
**Anyone can participate!** While the hackathon focuses on African impact, it's open to developers worldwide.

### Can I use existing code or projects?
**Partially.** Projects must be new or significantly updated after August 1, 2025. You can use open-source libraries and templates.

### What languages are supported?
**English** is required for submissions, but you can develop in any programming language with Hedera SDKs (JavaScript, Python, Go, Java, etc.).

### Is certification required to win prizes?
**Yes!** At least one member from your team must have completed the Hedera Developer Certification and earned a certificate to be eligible for hackathon prizes.

### How are winners selected?
**Judging Criteria:**
- Innovation & originality (30%)
- Real-world impact, especially for Africa (30%)
- Technical execution & Hedera usage (25%)
- Feasibility & scalability (15%)
